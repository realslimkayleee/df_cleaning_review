A Detailed Checklist for Every Room

Our standard cleaning service ensures your kitchen becomes a sparkling and hygienic space for you and your family. We meticulously clean and sanitize all countertops, wipe down cabinet exteriors, clean the stovetop, and polish the exterior of your appliances. We also thoroughly scrub and disinfect the sink and faucet, and finish by vacuuming and mopping the floor to leave it spotless.

In the bathrooms, our focus is on complete sanitation and shine. We deep clean and disinfect the toilet, shower, and tub, ensuring all soap scum and grime are removed. Sinks, vanities, and faucets are scrubbed and polished, mirrors are left streak-free, and all chrome fixtures are shined. We complete the process by emptying wastebaskets and thoroughly cleaning the floor.

For your living areas and bedrooms, we create a fresh and dust-free environment. Our team dusts all accessible surfaces, including windowsills, baseboards, and furniture. We carefully vacuum all carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture, and we mop all hard floors. We can also make beds and tidy up general clutter to restore a sense of order and tranquility to your personal spaces.
