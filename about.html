Our Story

We Are Passionate About Cleaning

Welcome to DF Cleaning! We are dedicated to transforming your spaces into pristine, welcoming environments. Our team of experienced cleaning professionals is committed to delivering outstanding results with every single visit.

DF Cleaning was founded with a simple goal: to provide high-quality, reliable, and thorough cleaning services for both residential homes and commercial businesses across Texas. We believe that a clean environment leads to a happier, healthier, and more productive life. That's why we focus on attention to detail, using the best products and techniques to ensure every corner is spotless.

Our Core Mission

To offer unmatched cleaning services that let you spend your time on what truly matters. We strive for excellence, integrity, and absolute customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity First: We build long-term relationships based on honesty, transparency, and trust.
  • Uncompromising Quality: We pay absolute attention to the details, leaving no spot untouched.
  • Reliability: We respect your schedule and show up on time, fully prepared to clean.

Providing premium residential and commercial cleaning services across Texas. Our reputation is spotless.
